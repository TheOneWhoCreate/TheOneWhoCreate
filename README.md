## 👋 Hi, I’m Atanu

I’m Atanu Rooj, an AEIE engineer who prefers building real systems over just talking about them. My work sits at the intersection of hardware, AI, and efficient computing — especially where constraints matter. I’ve spent a lot of time designing FPGA-based pipelines, working with Verilog, and optimizing systems using approximate computing techniques.

I also lead teams in hackathons, where I handle system architecture and hardware decisions while pushing execution forward.

I’m not someone who relies on flashy presentation — I care more about whether the system actually works, scales, and can be defended technically. That said, I’m actively improving how I communicate and present ideas, because I know execution alone isn’t enough.

My approach is simple: build fast, debug deeply, optimize relentlessly, and learn whatever is needed to make the system work.

📫 Always open to collaboration and learning.
